The Journey

Milestones
& Victories

2016
Company Founded
Launched Magadine in Thaba Chweu — 100% black woman owned from day one. A declaration of possibility.
2017
First Major Contracts
Supply agreements across Thaba Chweu and Greater Tubatse. The industry started paying attention.
2019
Manufacturing Launch
In-house manufacturing of hand cleaners and 1-ton textile bags. Building, not just delivering.
2021
B-BBEE Level 1 Certified
The highest possible rating. Not a checkbox — a covenant with transformation.
2023
National Media Recognition
Featured in Business Day, Mining Weekly. Her story became a mirror for every woman told she didn't belong.
2024+
Expanding to Construction
Diversifying into construction supply and scaling manufacturing nationally. The vision was never small.
