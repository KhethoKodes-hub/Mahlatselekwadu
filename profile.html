Her Story

Purpose
& Urgency
In Motion

"To deliver remarkable service with a purposeful sense of urgency — every order, every time."

Miss Mahlatse Lekwadu founded Magadine Business Enterprise in 2016 within the Thaba Chweu Municipality — not just as a business, but as proof that Black women belong at the helm of South Africa's most critical industries.

Where It Began

"I grew up watching the mines shape everything around us — the land, the livelihoods, the families. I knew that if this industry was going to transform, it would need people who truly understood what was at stake."

Her vision extends beyond supply chains. She is a living embodiment of B-BBEE transformation — actively reshaping what leadership looks like in mining supply, construction, and industrial services.

The Journey

Milestones
& Victories

Every year, a new chapter. Every milestone, a door opened for the women who come after.

2016
Company Founded
Launched Magadine Business Enterprise in Thaba Chweu Municipality — 100% black woman owned from day one. A statement of defiance. A declaration of possibility.
2017
First Major Contracts
Secured supply agreements with key mining operations across the Thaba Chweu and Greater Tubatse corridors. The industry started paying attention.
2019
Manufacturing Launch
Began in-house manufacturing of hand cleaners with grit and 1-ton textile bags — moving from pure supply to production. Building, not just delivering.
2021
B-BBEE Level 1 Certified
Achieved Level 1 B-BBEE contributor status — the highest possible rating. Not a checkbox. A covenant with transformation.
2023
National Media Recognition
Featured across Business Day, Forbes Africa, Mining Weekly and more. Her story became a mirror for every woman told she didn't belong in this industry.
2024+
Expanding to Construction
Next chapter underway — diversifying into construction supply and scaling manufacturing nationally. The vision was never small. It was only just beginning.
