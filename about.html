Our Leadership
Team

Two extraordinary women whose vision, resilience, and purpose built Magadine into what it is today.

Founder & Director
Miss Marumo Mkhonto
Sole Director · 51% Shareholder

With a vision to create opportunities in the mining supply sector, Miss Mkhonto founded Magadine Business Enterprise in 2016. Her strategic leadership and deep understanding of the industry have been instrumental in our growth and success.

Her commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives our company's mission to deliver remarkable service with a purposeful sense of urgency.

49% Shareholder
Co-owner & Strategic Partner
Ms. Mahlatse Lekwadu
Co-owner · 49% Shareholder

As a disabled black woman business owner, Ms. Lekwadu brings a unique perspective and resilience to our leadership team. Her partnership represents our commitment to inclusive empowerment and diverse representation in business leadership.

Her involvement ensures that Magadine remains grounded in the principles of accessibility, inclusion, and community empowerment.

Our Growth

The
Journey

2016
Company Foundation

Magadine Business Enterprise was founded in the Thaba Chweu Municipality area as a 100% black woman owned enterprise — born from vision, purpose, and an unwavering belief in transformation.

2017 – 2018
Expansion & Growth

Expanded the product range to include comprehensive PPE equipment and established partnerships with major mining operations across the Thaba Chweu and Greater Tubatse areas.

2019
Manufacturing Begins

Started manufacturing hand cleaners with grit and producing 1-ton textile bags to meet specific industry requirements, diversifying our offering beyond pure supply.

2020 – Present
Consolidation & Future Planning

Consolidated our position as a trusted supplier to mining operations while actively planning our expansion into construction services — the next chapter of the Magadine story.
