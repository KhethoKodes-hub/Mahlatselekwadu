Who We Are

Engineered for Safety & Reliability

Magadine Business Enterprise was founded in 2016 as a 100% black women-owned mining and industrial supply company. Based in the industrial heart of Steelpoort, Limpopo, we have grown into a respected partner for mining houses, municipalities, and industrial clients.

We supply high-quality Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), safety gear, hygiene products, and essential industrial consumables. Our mission is to deliver reliable supply chain solutions while championing inclusive economic participation in traditionally male-dominated sectors.

The Heart of Magadine

Her Story: Resilience Beyond Adversity

Mahlatse Lekwadu's journey — from personal hardship to business success — is the soul of our enterprise.

“My condition is not a disability — it is a different ability. Limitations can be transformed into purpose.”

Mahlatse Lekwadu
Entrepreneur | Co-Founder | MBA (2021)

Mahlatse Lekwadu is a South African entrepreneur and Co-Founder of Magadine Business Enterprise. At 53, she has built a reputation as a resilient and purpose-driven leader in a traditionally male-dominated sector. She holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Management and an MBA, which she completed in 2021 despite significant personal and health challenges.

Following the passing of her husband, who founded the business, she stepped in to lead and grow the company, transforming it into a trusted supplier with established industry relationships. Her leadership is grounded in discipline, faith, and a commitment to excellence and inclusive economic participation.

Mahlatse’s journey is deeply rooted in resilience. Raised in a challenging environment, she took on responsibilities at a young age, helping care for her siblings and contributing to her household. Later, she faced Guillain-Barré Syndrome, a neurological condition that affected her mobility. She was declared permanently incapacitated — a reality that took time to accept. Yet, through faith, perseverance, and rehabilitation, she rebuilt her strength, both physically and mentally.

“Not a disability, but a different ability” — this mindset guides her life and leadership.

Purpose-Driven Leadership

Mahlatse Lekwadu: MBA, Coach & Pioneer

Holding both a Postgraduate Diploma in Management and an MBA (completed in 2021) — achieved during significant health battles — Mahlatse exemplifies that resilience creates opportunity. As Co-Founder of Magadine Business Enterprise, she turned grief into growth after her husband’s passing, steering the company to become a formidable player in mining supply.

Her leadership is rooted in discipline, faith, and an unshakable commitment to inclusive economic participation. She often shares that her Guillain-Barré Syndrome diagnosis taught her to view “different ability” as strength. This philosophy permeates the entire company culture, inspiring employees, partners, and clients.
