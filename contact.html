Common Questions

Quick
Answers

How quickly do you respond to quotes?
We respond to all quote requests within 24 hours on business days. Urgent orders are prioritised — just note it in your message and we'll turn it around faster.
Do you deliver outside Steelpoort?
Yes — we supply and deliver across the Thaba Chweu and Greater Tubatse regions. For larger orders or special arrangements, contact us directly to discuss delivery options.
What is your minimum order quantity?
We cater to all order sizes — from single items to bulk site supply orders. There is no minimum order requirement. All orders receive the same level of care and urgency.
Are you B-BBEE compliant?
Yes — Magadine Business Enterprise is a certified B-BBEE Level 1 contributor and 100% Black Woman Owned enterprise, making us an ideal procurement partner for transformation targets.
Can you source products not in your catalogue?
Absolutely. If the item isn't in our standard catalogue, we source on demand for our partners. Contact us with your specifications and we'll find a solution.
Do you offer credit terms or accounts?
We offer account facilities for established clients and approved mining houses. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we'll tailor a payment arrangement that works for both parties.
