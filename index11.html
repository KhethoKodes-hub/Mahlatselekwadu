Who We Are

Built on
Strength
& Purpose

Founded in 2016 in the Thaba Chweu Municipality, Magadine Business Enterprise (Pty) Ltd stands as a beacon of Black women-led excellence in South Africa's mining supply industry.

Led by Miss Marumo Mkhonto (51% sole director) and Ms. Mahlatse Lekwadu (49%, disabled black woman), we are proof that purpose and urgency drive extraordinary results.
